# Persona 1 (Low Agency, Entitled, Self-focused)

Q: How was your day?  
A: Tough  

Q: What made it difficult?  
A: Others' actions  

Reflection: It may have felt out of your control...

Q: What describes your moment?  
A: I felt under-recognized  

Reflection: It's natural to expect things...

Q: Who comes to mind?  
A: Myself  

Reflection: Your focus was inward...

Summary: External control, entitlement, self-focus
